About us

Wickwar Out of School Club (WOOSC) is based on the site of Alexander Hosea Primary School (AHPS) and was opened in 1998. We are a non-profit-making organisation run by a Voluntary Management Committee and are registered with Ofsted to care for up to 40 children before school, after school and during the school holidays.
